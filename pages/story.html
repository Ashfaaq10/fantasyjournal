Arc 1 : Childhood Traumas

After witnessing the death of his beloved mother at the hand of his own father, Lucien is forced out of the estate to go serve at the black castle to defend against creatures.

Arc 2 : Young Adventurer

After getting an ultimatum fromhis father that at age 15, he were obligated to join the magic academy and later serve the estate and the continent, Lucien decides to spend whatever few years of freedom he has left to wander and philander.

Arc 3 : Bloody School Life

School life does not entertain Lucien who is still far away from his revenge.So he will do anything necessary to get out at any means.

Arc 4 : Intercontinental Tournament

As punishment for his actions, Lucien becomes a slave to the Principal and is forced to participate in a tournament that will determine the future balance of power.

Arc 5 : Joining the demons

After a shocking revelation, Lucien joins hands with the enemy to accomplish his revenge.That will entail some difficult choices in becoming a traitor.

Arc 6 : Invasion against Home

After being lackey to the demons, Lucien proceeds to the estate to confront his father and maybe acheive his lifelong goal.Or will he?

Arc 7 : Life after Death

After seemingly killing himself to erase the demons from his estate, Lucien wakes up ashore a lake where seems to be a whole different continent all together.

Arc 8 : Serving the Queen

The queen has something of Lucien's and forces him to work for her as a personal knight to invade the neighbouring country.

Arc 9 : Fighting a Losing Battle

Losing is inevitable after the enemy garners surprising yet familiar allies.In a last ditch attempt, Lucien saves the Queen and escapes.

Arc 10 : Wanderers

No Home and No one Else.Lucien and the Queen find solace in each other as they wander the world.
