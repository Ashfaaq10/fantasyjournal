Arc 1 : Childhood Traumas

An enigmatic warrior takes an interest in Lucien and offers to train him in secret, promising to help him achieve his revenge one day.

Lucien forms an unlikely friendship with another outcast at the Black Castle, someone who harbors their own vendetta against the establishment.

A veteran knight at the Black Castle takes a dislike to Lucien and challenges him to a series of increasingly dangerous trials, claiming it’s to toughen him up.

Lucien finds an old heirloom belonging to his mother, which seems to possess strange powers.

Lucien discovers that someone within the Black Castle is feeding information to the creatures, causing the defenses to fail at critical moments.

A pack of wolves has taken up residence near the castle’s outskirts, threatening the safety of those who venture out. Lucien is tasked with eliminating the wolves and securing the area.

The castle’s healer is running low on a rare herb that grows in a dangerous part of the forest. Lucien is sent to gather a sufficient amount of the herb before the next attack on the castle.

A group of merchants is delivering supplies to the Black Castle, but the route they must take is dangerous. Lucien is assigned to help protect the caravan from potential threats.

After a recent attack, the outer walls of the Black Castle have been damaged. Lucien is tasked with helping the masons gather stone from a nearby quarry to repair the walls.

Inhabitants of the castle have reported hearing strange noises coming from a nearby abandoned outpost. Lucien is sent to investigate and determine if there is any danger.

A nearby farmer is struggling with a pest problem that’s destroying his crops. Lucien is asked to help by clearing out the pests and ensuring the farmer can harvest his crops safely.

A stray dog has been spotted near the castle, and some of the inhabitants are worried it might attract unwanted attention from larger predators. Lucien is asked to find the dog and bring it back to safety.

A message needs to be delivered to a neighboring village or outpost, but the usual messengers are unavailable. Lucien is asked to take on the task.

A group of scavengers has been looting the outskirts of the Black Castle, stealing supplies and causing trouble. Lucien is sent to scare them off or deal with them.

A band of goblins has been raiding nearby villages and stealing supplies. Lucien is sent to track them down and stop the raids.

A remote outpost under the Black Castle’s control is under siege by a powerful enemy force. Lucien is sent to reinforce the outpost and hold off the attackers until reinforcements arrive.

The Black Castle’s scholars have located an ancient relic buried deep within a dangerous dungeon. Lucien is tasked with retrieving the relic before it falls into the wrong hands.

The Disappearance Description: A fellow member of the Black Castle, Sir Roderick, has not returned from his patrol in the forest. The castle is concerned, and Lucien is asked to venture into the forest to find him. Gameplay: Lucien follows tracks and signs of struggle through the forest. Along the way, he encounters increasingly hostile creatures, hinting that something is disturbing the natural balance. The Dark Secret Description: As Lucien investigates, he discovers evidence of Sir Roderick's experimentation with monster blood. Old journals or notes reveal that Roderick believed ingesting the blood would give him the strength to defend the castle, but the blood instead cursed him. Gameplay: Lucien finds a hidden campsite where Roderick conducted his experiments. Clues like discarded vials, bloodstained tools, and torn clothing lead Lucien to realize that Roderick has transformed into a werewolf. The Hunt Begins Description: Lucien tracks the werewolf deeper into the forest, discovering that the recent increase in monster attacks is due to Roderick’s transformation. Roderick, now consumed by his curse, has been leading the monsters in attacks against the castle and nearby villages. Gameplay: This part of the quest involves tracking the werewolf through the forest, battling other creatures drawn to Roderick's newfound power. The tension builds as Lucien gets closer to confronting the werewolf. The Confrontation Description: Lucien finally confronts Roderick, now fully transformed into a powerful werewolf. Despite Roderick's bestial nature, there may be moments of lucidity where he pleads with Lucien to end his suffering. Gameplay: This is a challenging boss fight where Lucien must defeat Roderick. The werewolf will have enhanced strength, speed, and regenerative abilities, making it a tough battle. Lucien might have the option to use silver weapons or other special items to weaken the werewolf. The Aftermath Description: After defeating the werewolf, Lucien returns to the Black Castle with the grim news. The castle members mourn the loss of Sir Roderick, but they are also relieved that the attacks will cease. This event might leave Lucien with a sense of guilt or questions about the dangers of power and the consequences of desperation. Gameplay: The quest concludes with Lucien reporting back to the castle. He might receive a reward, but the emotional weight of the encounter could influence his future decisions.

The Omen Description: The Black Castle receives word from the castle’s scholars and seers that "The Long Night" is approaching—a rare event that occurs once every 100 years when the moon eclipses the sun, plunging the world into darkness. During this time, an ancient prophecy warns of a monstrous horde rising from the icy wilderness, intent on overwhelming the world of men. Gameplay: Lucien and the castle’s inhabitants prepare for the impending assault. This stage involves fortifying the castle's defenses, gathering supplies, and boosting morale among the soldiers. There might also be smaller skirmishes or scouting missions to gather intelligence on the approaching threat. The Darkness Descends Description: As the eclipse begins, the temperature plummets, and darkness falls over the land. From the frozen forests and mountains, the monster horde emerges—an overwhelming force of ice wraiths, frost giants, and other winter-born creatures. The Black Castle's walls are the only thing standing between these creatures and the human lands beyond. Gameplay: This stage involves defending the castle's walls and gates. Lucien and his fellow soldiers fight off wave after wave of monsters. The battle is chaotic, with Lucien having to move between different parts of the castle to reinforce defenses, assist wounded comrades, and repel the relentless assault. The Commander’s Stand Description: As the battle rages on, it becomes clear that the monsters are being driven by a powerful leader—a Frost Wyrm or an ancient Ice Lich. The castle’s commander, a seasoned warrior and tactician, leads a counteroffensive, rallying the soldiers and pushing back against the tide of monsters. Gameplay: In this stage, Lucien fights alongside the commander in a series of critical battles. The commander provides strategic guidance and support, turning the tide of battle with his leadership. Lucien might be tasked with special missions during the fight, such as disabling siege weapons or taking down key enemy leaders. The Final Push Description: In a desperate final assault, the monster horde throws everything they have at the Black Castle. The battle reaches its peak as Lucien and the commander face the monstrous leader in a climactic showdown. Gameplay: This is the final boss battle of the quest. Lucien, the commander, and the remaining soldiers must defeat the Frost Wyrm or Ice Lich leading the horde. The battle is intense, with the enemy using powerful ice-based magic and devastating attacks. Victory requires teamwork, quick reflexes, and strategic thinking. The Dawn of Victory Description: After the battle, the moon passes, and the sun slowly returns, bringing an end to "The Long Night." The monster horde is defeated, and the Black Castle stands victorious but battered. Lucien and his comrades are exhausted but alive, having defended the castle and the realm from certain destruction. Gameplay: The aftermath involves tending to the wounded, mourning the fallen, and repairing the castle’s defenses. Lucien reflects on the battle and the sacrifices made. This stage also serves as a moment of character growth for Lucien, solidifying his resolve and preparing him for the challenges ahead. The Summons Description: A week after the battle, Lucien is summoned to the estate by his father. He is to attend a ceremony with the king, where he will be commended for his bravery and efforts in defending the realm. This event is both an honor and a reminder of the obligations that await him, setting the stage for the next arc. Gameplay: Lucien travels to the estate, where he is greeted with respect and admiration. The ceremony is a formal affair, with the king and other nobles in attendance. Lucien might receive a special reward or title, but the experience also serves as a reminder of the weight of duty and the expectations placed upon him by his father and the kingdom.
